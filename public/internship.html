Anita Devi Memorial Education and Welfare Society

ADM Education & Welfare Society stands as a beacon of hope, dedicated to Empowering Change and Transforming Lives through its multifaceted initiatives. At the heart of our organization lies an unwavering commitment to create positive and lasting impacts, driven by fresh perspectives and innovative ideas.

Rooted in the belief that change starts with education, ADM Society focuses on holistic development across various sectors. Our initiatives encompass a wide spectrum of areas, ranging from Environmental Sustainability and Rural Development to Women's Empowerment and Health Awareness.

Job description:

Project Manager:

Project Managers play a crucial role in guiding and motivating project teams to achieve project objectives. They are responsible for identifying project scope, budgets, schedules, and risks. Additionally, they develop and execute project plans, monitoring progress and making necessary adjustments.

Graphic Designer:

Graphic Designers use software like Adobe Photoshop to create visual concepts that inspire and engage users. They design elements such as logos, images, and illustrations, contributing creative ideas to various departments for design concepts and layouts.

Medical Volunteer:

Medical Volunteers offer essential support in healthcare facilities, clinics, or outreach programs. They provide medical care, raise awareness about health issues, and assist healthcare professionals in delivering treatments and performing medical procedures.

Video Editor:

Video Editors creatively edit video content for publication on social platforms. They assemble raw footage, select shots based on quality and content relevance, and add music, dialogues, graphics, and effects for a polished final product.

UI/UX Designer:

UI/UX Designers excel in creating user-friendly interfaces by collecting user requirements and conducting research. Their aim is to deliver outstanding user experiences through innovative designs.

Marketing:

Marketers contribute ideas, conduct marketing campaigns, devise effective strategies, and carry out research to promote products or services.

Campus Ambassador:

Campus Ambassadors display leadership skills by promoting an organization on campus. They create ideas for events and campaigns, fostering collaborations with firms and colleges.

Charity Intern:

Charity Interns possess communication skills and work on encouraging donations and fundraising activities to support charitable causes.

Full Stack Developers:

Full Stack Developers handle both frontend and backend responsibilities. Frontend developers create user-friendly and responsive websites, while backend developers build APIs and manage data, along with communication, testing, debugging, and database management.

Anchor:

Anchors create and deliver presentations while coordinating events. They maintain a positive attitude, engage the audience, and exhibit good communication skills.

Public Relations:

Professionals in Public Relations write and produce presentations, articles, press releases, and media posts. They handle public and press inquiries, speak at press conferences, and analyze media coverage.

Human Resources:

Human Resources professionals support HR initiatives, provide counseling on policies and procedures, implement effective onboarding plans, and develop training and development programs.

CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility):

CSR personnel identify, measure, and monitor the social impact of an organization's activities. They influence the organization's culture by identifying CSR initiatives and engage with external bodies like charities and community groups.

These internship roles are designed to empower students, housewives, and individuals from all backgrounds to develop valuable skills and gain practical experience. Your achievements and contributions are our inspiration, and together, we can work towards shaping a more educated, empowered, and capable world.
